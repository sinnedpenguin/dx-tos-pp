Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

1.1. DennX stores chat history in a database to maintain a conversational context during user interactions. This includes messages exchanged with the bot. You can delete your chat history by using /clear. For one-time questions without storage, use /ask or /a.

2. Use of Collected Information

2.1. The stored chat history is used to maintain a conversational context and improve the quality of interactions. No user data is shared or used for operational purposes, troubleshooting, or any other purposes.

3. Data Security

3.1. While no user data is collected, we take reasonable measures to ensure the security and privacy of stored chat history.

4. Cookies

4.1. DennX does not use cookies. You can enjoy our services without concerns about cookie-related tracking.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

7. Contact Information
